Privacy Policy

GridLead helps you discover leads, send outreach, and track replies. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how you can control it.

What we collect

  • Account info: name, email, workspace settings.
  • Lead data you create/import (business names, notes, scoring).
  • Connected Gmail info (only if you connect it): tokens needed to send/receive outreach, thread/metadata used for replies and sync, status of the connection.
  • Product analytics and logs for reliability and abuse prevention.

How we use data

  • To operate the app: discovery, scoring, outreach sending, reply tracking, notifications.
  • To secure the platform and prevent abuse.
  • To provide support and improve features.

How we store and share

  • Data is stored in Supabase (Postgres) and processed via our Edge Functions.
  • Email delivery/notifications may use SendGrid/Resend.
  • We do not sell personal data. We only share with service providers needed to run GridLead.

Gmail integration specifics

  • We use OAuth; we never see your password.
  • We access Gmail only to send outreach, track replies, and monitor connection health.
  • Disconnect anytime in Settings or by revoking access in your Google Account.

Your controls

  • Disconnect Gmail and delete lead data from your workspace.
  • Contact support to request deletion of your account data.

Security

We use HTTPS, role-based access, and least-privilege service accounts. Tokens are stored securely; we monitor for abuse.

Contact

Questions or requests: support@gridlead.space
